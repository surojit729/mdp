About MDP Recruitment

Welcome to MDP Recruitment the home of temporary and permanent recruitment working in partnership with our clients and our colleagues to achieve maximum results.

Offering both temporary and permanent staffing solutions through an independent search and selection process to source the right candidates for your business, our team are versatile and imaginative with a firm understanding of every type of working enjoyment. We have experience and specialize in Industrial, Recycling, Manufacturing, Logistics and Produce recruitment along with Permanente search and selection process for all business types.
